Hi! I am Alvin Wen

I’ve been in the hair industry since 2004 and I’ve never lost my passion about doing hair. I’ve worked in different salons and have been to different education classes, including Vidal Sassoon, Tony and Guys. I also attend fashion shows every year to ensure that your hair styles will be up to date. I specialize in haircuts, colors from basic one color to muti-colors, highlights and color correction, styles from young to sophisticated, trendy fashion to formal, classic. I also do hair extensions. When you sit on my chair, it’s my job to make you look great in your new hairstyle.

The products I work with are Wella, American Crew and SexyHair.
